Working notes
Constitution · System sketches · Field log

The arboreum collects experiments in orchestrating computational work across long horizons: drafting constitutions for systems, encoding provenance, and designing interfaces that expose structure rather than hide it.

The emphasis is on **durability** over novelty: fewer systems, better tended, whose behaviour can be inspected, explained, and amended without losing their history.

Current lines of work
  • HENDECAD system: orchestration layer for accountable AI workflows.
  • Constitutional templates for personal and small‑team tooling.
  • Symbolic archives linking documents, diagrams, and decision logs.
